Our Plan

Your Loved Items, Our Careful Eye

At Tess Arnold Organization we help people who are looking to make life simpler, so you and your family can more easily enjoy the next stage of life. We specialize in helping people declutter, downsize, and digitize their stuff. We start with the places that are the least sentimental like storage units, attics, garages, cellars, and closets. We then move on to clothes, books, and furniture. We save the special trinkets, photos, and paper for last. We love working with people who are looking to:

  • Downsize their home or move to retirement communities
  • Clear their kids’ stuff out now that they are empty nesters
  • Clean up after themselves so their loved ones don’t have to when they are gone
  • Prepare extra rooms for new additions to the family
Our approach is less about cute baskets and more about practically editing down possessions. Our goal is to recycle, rehome, repurpose, or reuse the treasured items that you simply don’t need anymore.
